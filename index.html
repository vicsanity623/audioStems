The Meta Demucs Revolution

For decades, audio engineers viewed the separation of a fully mixed track into its original stems as the "holy grail" of signal processing. Traditional methods relied on phase cancellation and aggressive EQ filtering, which inevitably left audio riddled with underwater artifacts.
